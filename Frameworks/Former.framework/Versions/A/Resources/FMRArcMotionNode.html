Arc Motion

The Arc Motion patch moves views along an arc, which is a common pattern in Material Design.

  • X Position Begin
    The X position at the beginning of the animation.
  • Y Position Begin
    The Y position at the beginning of the animation.
  • X Position End
    The X position at the end of the animation.
  • Y Position End
    The Y position at the end of the animation.
  • Min Angle
    The minimum arc along the circle between two points aligned near horizontally.
  • Max Angle
    The maximum angle of the arc on a circle describing the path between the start and end points.
  • Flipped
    Inverts the circle on which the arc lies.
  • Progress
    A number from 0-1 that represents the progress of the animation.
  • X Position
    The X position at the current spot in the transition.
  • Y Position
    The Y position at the current spot in the transition.
