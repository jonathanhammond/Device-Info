Simple Take Picture

The Simple Spring patch renders the device's camera as an image and supports taking a picture.

  • Camera
    You can choose either the front or back camera. As the device can only use one camera at a time, this is a signal input.
  • Take Picture
    A signal input that instructs the patch to take a picture of the current frame.
  • Reset
    A signal input that resets the patch back to outputting the camera preview.
  • Size
    The size of the image image.
  • Image
    The image containing either the live camera preview or the picture that was taken.
