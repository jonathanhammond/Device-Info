Pinch Gesture

The Pinch Gesture patch recognizes a two finger pinch gesture on a view. Upon recognizing that a pinch gesture has occurred, it will cancel out all other interactions on the view or its subviews.

Pinch gestures are commonly used to scale a view, and will retain the scale value after the gesture is complete.

  • Reset
    Resets the gesture.
  • Scale
    The initial scale.
  • Gesture
    The gesture connection port used to link the gesture to a view.
  • Scale
    The current scale of the pinch gesture.
  • Active
    A boolean value that represents whether the patch has recognized a pinch gesture.
